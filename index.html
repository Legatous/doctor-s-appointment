Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions about our clinic and services.

  • Is there any consultation fee?

    There is no consultation fee for your initial visit. We offer a complimentary consultation to assess your needs and discuss potential treatments.

  • How do I schedule an appointment?

    You can schedule an appointment by calling our clinic or using our online booking system on our website. We strive to accommodate your preferred time and date.

  • What insurance plans do you accept?

    We accept a wide range of insurance plans. Please contact our office, and our staff will be happy to assist you in verifying your insurance coverage.

  • Do you offer virtual consultations?

    Yes, we offer virtual consultations for certain services. Please contact our office to schedule a virtual appointment and discuss your specific needs.

  • How do I prepare for my appointment?

    Before your appointment, please ensure you bring any relevant medical records or test results. Follow any pre-appointment instructions provided by our staff.
